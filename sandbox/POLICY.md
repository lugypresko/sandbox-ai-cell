AI-CELL -- Governance \& Decision Policy



1\. Policy Scope



This policy governs what AI-CELL is allowed to do inside the sandbox.



Default mode: Observe \& Recommend

Action is optional and must be explicitly enabled.



2\. Authority Levels

Level 0 -- Observe



AI-CELL may:



Read signals



Learn baselines



Infer states



Log insights



AI-CELL may not:



Recommend actions



Trigger alerts



Change anything



Level 1 -- Recommend



AI-CELL may:



Propose actions



Rank alternatives



Explain tradeoffs



AI-CELL may not:



Execute actions



Chain decisions automatically



Level 2 -- Act (Optional, Explicit)



AI-CELL may:



Execute predefined actions



Only within declared blast radius



Only if confidence threshold is met



Any Level 2 action must:



Reference a specific policy clause



Be reversible



Be logged with justification



3\. Decision Constraints



Every decision must include:



Triggering signal(s)



Current state



Policy reference



Confidence score



Expected impact



If any field is missing  decision is invalid.



4\. Forbidden Behaviors



AI-CELL must never:



Invent missing data



Optimize for "quiet dashboards"



Escalate without signal evidence



Adapt policy implicitly



Policy evolution is human-only.



5\. Failure Mode



When uncertain:



Prefer inaction



Log uncertainty



Request human review



Silence is safer than a wrong action.

Defines authority, boundaries, and time governance.
